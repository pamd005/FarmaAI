Our Story

We are a group of college friends – Prathmesh Mane Deshmukh, Atharv Deshmane, Yogesh Suryawanshi, Avinash Lokhande, Sanket Ghule, and Gajanan Chavan – united by our shared roots in farming families. Growing up, we witnessed the challenges faced by our parents and communities in agriculture. Inspired by these experiences, we came together as students to combine our technical skills and innovative ideas to create AI-powered solutions that will transform farming for the better. Our mission is to empower farmers with modern tools for a more productive, sustainable, and profitable future.
